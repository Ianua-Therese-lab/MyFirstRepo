Brief Introduction About Myself
Ianua-Therese Duru is a wellness strategist, content writer, and community advocate committed to helping people live healthier and more intentional lives. With a foundation in Biochemistry/Microbiology and experience as a Medical Laboratory Scientist, Wellness Consultant, and Strategic Communications professional, she blends science, storytelling, and strategy to simplify complex health topics and inspire positive behavior change.
Over the past five years, she has collaborated with organizations such as the Federal Ministry of Environment, Doctor’s Health Initiative, Naomi Lucas Co., and the Focolare Movement to advance holistic health, sustainability, and youth development. She now focuses on wellness content writing, youth empowerment, and sustainable development—driven by service, integrity, and the desire to create meaningful impact in every community she serves.
